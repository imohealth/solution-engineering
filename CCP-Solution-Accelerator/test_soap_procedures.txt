SUBJECTIVE:
Patient is a 65-year-old female presenting with worsening knee pain.

OBJECTIVE:
Physical examination shows decreased range of motion in right knee.
X-ray shows severe osteoarthritis.

ASSESSMENT:
Severe osteoarthritis of right knee
Chronic pain

PLAN:
1. Total knee replacement scheduled
2. Arthroscopy of right knee to evaluate cartilage damage
3. Physical therapy pre-operatively
4. Pain management with NSAIDs
